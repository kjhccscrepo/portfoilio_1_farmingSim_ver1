# Farming Simulator (C++)
This is a project for csc122 at Harper College.
### You begin the game with a limited supply of seeds, and can get more seeds by growing crops
## Controls
- You can move around with WASD.
- You can view your inventory with "i"
- You can plant your current selected seed with "p"
- You can change your selected seed with "o"
- You can harvest the crop with "h" (if it's grown)
  - *make sure to pull those pesky weeds too!*
- You can view controls in game with "l"
- You can quit the game with "q"
- You (currently) end the day by with "e"
- You can water the current plot you're on with "q"
- *but only when you have enough water*
- *(it resets at the end of the day)*
- You can go to the shop by with "m"
  - *you can buy seeds of plants you have, and new ones as the game progresses*
  - *also upgrades to the capacity of your watering can*
  - *and sell your crops for money to buy things*
- You can exit most sub menus by with "r"
